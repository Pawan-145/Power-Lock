How Our project works?

  1. You have to click on sustainability tracker option
  2. You have to select state
  3. Then fill form containing details like (FirstName,LastName,Email,Contact Number,state,Electricity per unit(KWh))
  4. Result page will appear.
  5. Result page will show calculated cost according to state and then give feedback whether it is Good or Bad

Our Vision

Our Vision is to make people aware of the energy usage pattern they are following currently by calculating the consumption of electrical energy by a user in one month and then rating them accordingly. Also providing them information about how they can reduce the excess energy usage and hence saving the energy.
